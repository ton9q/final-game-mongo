MY GAME

About Game

MY GAME is a fighting game in which you must fight with computer using your very own deadly warrior and your awesome brain. Before you enter the arena you can choose location, customise the appearance of your warrior and give it a name.

Once you have created your warrior you can start fighting! Push button attack and solve mini tasks to defeat enemies who are trying to kill you. This game is a huge amount of fun and you will find yourself re-entering the arena time and time again to beat your personal records and training your brain!
